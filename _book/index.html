David McCormick

2026-01-04

1 Nine years and rolling

This year marked my 9th Pan Mass Challenge (PMC) ride to support the Jimmy Fund and Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. Despite a demanding work schedule, I was blessed to be joined by my daughter, Ellen, and two of my best friends: college friend, Ed Kenealy, and my PhD advisor and long-time friend, John Grotzinger. For Ed, it was his 12th Pan Mass and the first time for John.

I continue to be part of the fabulous Team Crank, that provides an incredible match to our fundraising from the Warren Alpert Foundation, this year to the tune of $750,000. The team is raised the full amount for the match and we donated over $1.5 million to Dana-Farber. All of your contributions were doubled, which meant I was able to contribute nearly $40,000. I am incredibly grateful to everyone who supported me.

What follows is my attempt to evoke the sights, sounds, and feelings of something so worthwhile and larger than myself. Join me.

David
